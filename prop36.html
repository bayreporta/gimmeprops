Prop 36 would reform the 18-year-old “three strikes law” for a person convicted of a third strike. Unless you commit a serious, violent felony, or the crime falls into one of the listed exceptions, a third striker would only get double the usual sentence for a crime instead of the 25 years to life set in the current three strikes law.

- Nightshade, Serial Inmate and Prison Expert
